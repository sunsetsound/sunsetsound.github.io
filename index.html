Sunset Sounds: Who We Are

We are a small, local business located in San Francisco (and sometimes in Irvine!), California dedicated to building speakers and everything audio related. Sunset Sounds provides handcrafted custom portable speakers and other audio devices that feature new technologies that redesign portable bluetooth speakers and deliver premium sound experience while you relax on a beautiful California beach or unwind with hot shower after a long day. In addition to our custom-designed speakers, we also provide vehicular and home audio services and solutions that are sure to have your vehicle bouncing to the beat or have the landlord banging on your door. Whatever the case may be, Sunset Sounds aims to put some butt-kicking bass and an awesome concert listening experience wherever you want and wherever you go.

Custom speakers never sounded this good.

Sunset Sounds makes customizing your own speaker easier than ever. We have speakers for those on the go or at home. Have ridiculous battery life, or a fast charging battery. Portable or stationary. Smooth frequency response, ear-piercing trebles, or soul-shattering bass. Bluetooth, AUX, or even guitar inputs. Smart displays for battery life and info. Smart speakers with remote turn on. RGB sound reactive LEDs, no need to explain how dope those are. The choice is yours, and we are hear you. Experience music the way you want.

Or choose one of Sunset Sounds' tried and true Signature models.

Unsatisfied with the existing portable speakers on the market, we set out to create a formula for our ideal portable bluetooth speaker. After years of research, prototyping, and engineering, we have finally perfected our design. Our Signature and Signature SE speakers will make just about any other portable bluetooth speaker you've used feel like a toy with our exceptional audio quality, extended bass response, and high sound intensity. These speakers are equipped with an arsenal of technological enhancements such as: aptX/AAC Bluetooth, IoT connectivity, remote turn on from smart devices, real-time battery life estimates and monitoring, a large battery capacity, and last but not least, RGB sound reactive LEDs to take the party with you. In addition, we craft our speakers with aesthetics and handfeel in mind by using wood and aluminum as our primary materials. The result is a premium portable speaker with a contemporary, eye-catching design and armed with a boat load of features and an unbelievable listening experience.

The Signature and Signature SE Models are produced quarterly due to availability.
