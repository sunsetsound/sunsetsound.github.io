Sunset Sounds

We are a small, local buisness located in San Francisco (and sometimes in Irvine!), California dedicated to building speakers and everything audio related. Sunset Sounds seeks to bring handcrafted custom portable speakers and other audio devices that feature new technologies that redesign portable bluetooth speakers and deliver premium sound experience while you relax on a beautiful California beach or unwind with hot shower after a long day. In addition to our custom-designed speakers, we also provide vehicular and home audio services and solutions that are sure to have your vehicle bouncing to the beat or have the landlord banging on your door. Whatever the case may be, Sunset Sounds aims to put some butt-kicking bass and an awesome concert listening experience wherever you want and wherever you go
