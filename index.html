Sunset Sounds: Who We Are

We are a small, local business located in San Francisco (and sometimes in Irvine!), California, dedicated to building the best speakers and everything audio related. Sunset Sounds produces handcrafted, custom speakers and other audio devices, featuring new technologies that redesign portable bluetooth speakers. Our speakers provide a premium listening experience while you relax on a beautiful California beach or unwind in a hot shower after a long day. In addition to our custom-designed speakers, we also provide vehicular and home audio solutions that are sure to have your vehicle bouncing to the beat or the landlord banging on your door. Whatever the case may be, Sunset Sounds aims to put some butt-kicking bass and an awesome concert listening experience wherever you want or wherever you go!

Choose one of Sunset Sounds' tried and true Signature SE speakers.

Unsatisfied with the existing portable speakers on the market? We were too. We set out to create a formula for our ideal portable bluetooth speaker. After years of research, prototyping, and engineering, we have finally perfected our design. Bluetooth speakers you’ve used in the past will feel like toys in comparison to our Signature and Signature SE speakers. Not only do they offer exceptional audio quality, extended bass response, and high sound intensity, but these speakers are also equipped with an arsenal of technological enhancements. These include, but are not limited to, aptX/AAC Bluetooth, IoT connectivity, remote turn on from smart devices, real-time battery life estimates and monitoring, a large battery capacity, and last but not least, RGB sound reactive LEDs (to take the party with you). In addition, we craft our speakers with aesthetics and handfeel in mind━its structure mainly comprised of wood and aluminum. This balance of beauty and functionality results in a premium bluetooth portable speaker with a contemporary, eye-catching design and numerous features that deliver an impressive listening experience.

The Signature and Signature SE Models are produced quarterly due to availability.
